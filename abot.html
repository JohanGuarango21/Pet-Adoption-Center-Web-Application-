About

Keep Animals Safe

Dedicated to Helping Every Animal Flourish

At Adopt Us, we are committed to ensuring that every animal in our care has the opportunity to thrive. We believe that every pet deserves not only a safe haven but also the love and support needed to reach their full potential. Our dedicated team provides individualized attention, medical care, and enrichment activities to help each animal flourish physically, emotionally, and socially. We focus on rehabilitation, training, and behavioral support to prepare our pets for successful adoptions into loving homes. Through community education and outreach, we aim to promote responsible pet ownership and advocate for the well-being of all animals. Together, we can create a nurturing environment where every pet can grow, heal, and find the forever home they deserve.

Our History

Adopt Us was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need. Established in 2002, our organization began as a small group of passionate animal lovers dedicated to making a difference in the lives of abandoned and neglected pets. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted community resource, expanding our reach and impact through our commitment to animal welfare. From our humble beginnings, we have successfully placed thousands of animals into loving homes, working tirelessly to raise awareness about the importance of adoption. Our shelter has evolved to include a wide range of services, including medical care, behavioral training, and educational programs for the community. As we continue to grow, our dedication to helping every animal flourish remains at the heart of everything we do. Together, with the support of our volunteers and donors, we strive to create a brighter future for all animals in our care..






Our Team

At Adopt Us, our team is a passionate and dedicated group of animal lovers committed to making a positive impact in the lives of every pet we serve. Comprised of experienced staff, skilled volunteers, and enthusiastic community members, we work collaboratively to provide the best care and support for our animals. Each team member brings unique skills and expertise, from animal care and training to community outreach and administration. Together, we strive to create a welcoming environment that fosters compassion, understanding, and love for all animals.
